    • The General Academic Strand (GAS) is designed for students who have a wide range of interests and may not have yet decided on a specific career path. It provides a comprehensive education that emphasizes critical thinking, communication skills, and a strong foundation in various academic disciplines. Students in the GAS strand are encouraged to explore different subjects, develop analytical abilities, and prepare for further studies in college.
